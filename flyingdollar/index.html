Flying Dollar Airport

All pilots and students are welcome at the oldest airport in the Poconos (formerly Barrett Airport, est. 1927). The Flying Dollar (8N4) is a privately-owned, public-use airport adjacent to the famed Skytop Lodge. Its well-maintained turf airstrip, marked by easy-to-see white plastic cones, is 2405' long and 100' wide on a heading of 02/20.

Landings on Rwy 02, departures on Rwy 20 only. Use CTAF 122.9. No fuel service but tie-down anchors are available if you bring your own ropes. Daytime VFR only. Come say hello!
